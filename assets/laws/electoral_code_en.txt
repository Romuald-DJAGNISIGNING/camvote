CAMVOTE - Cameroon Electoral Code Highlights (Unofficial Summary)

Highlights:
1) Voting age and suffrage
   - The vote is equal and secret.
   - Every citizen aged 20 and above is entitled to vote.

2) Eligibility to vote
   - Cameroonian citizens (male or female) who are registered on an electoral register and
     not disqualified by law may vote.

3) Electoral registers
   - Revision is the clean-up and update of existing registers.
   - Recompilation means fresh enrolment of all electors.

4) Annual revision period
   - ELECAM notes the annual revision runs from January 1 to August 31,
     unless the electoral college is convened.

5) Secret ballot
   - Secret ballot is individual voting where no elector is aware of another voter's choice.

6) Voting abroad
   - Embassy notices require a passport, a consular card, and a residence permit/resident card.
   - Requirements can vary by mission.

Notes:
- This summary is informational and does not replace the full Electoral Code.
- Always consult official ELECAM publications and legal texts.
