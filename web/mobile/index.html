Frequently asked

Quick answers for citizens and first-time voters.

Is the app official?

Yes. CamVote consolidates official civic sources and verified election updates.

Is my vote private?

Your vote is protected with device-bound cryptography and never shared publicly.

Can I vote from abroad?

Yes. The app includes diaspora polling centers and embassy locations.

What if my card expires?

You will receive renewal warnings and guided steps to update your card.
